  8. Information about cookies
    1. The Website uses cookies.
    2. Cookies are computer data in the form of small text files, which are stored on the Website user's terminal equipment and have been designed to facilitate browsing the Website. Cookies usually contain the name of the webpage they come from, the time of their storage on the terminal equipment and a unique number.
    3. The entity placing cookies on the terminal equipment of the Website user and obtaining access to them is the Website Operator.
    4. Cookies are used for the following purposes:
      1. Maintaining the Website user's session (after logging in); this way the user does not have to enter a login and password on every subpage of the Website
      2. Achieving the objectives set out above under ‘Relevant marketing techniques’
    5. Two fundamental types of cookies are used on the Website, i.e. ‘session cookies’ and ‘persistent cookies’. Session cookies are temporary files which are stored on the user's terminal equipment until logging out, leaving the Website or closing software (web browser). Persistent cookies are stored on the user's terminal equipment for the time set in the parameters of the cookies or until they are deleted by the user.
    6. The software designed for browsing web pages (web browser) usually allows storing cookies on the user's terminal equipment by default. Website users may change the settings in this respect at any time. A web browser enables users to delete cookies. It is also possible to automatically block cookies. Detailed information in this respect can be found in the help section or in the documentation of a given web browser.
    7. Limiting the use of cookies may affect some functionalities available within the Website.
    8. Cookies placed in the Website user's terminal equipment may also be used by advertisers and partners who cooperate with the Website Operator, including in particular Google (Google Inc., USA), Facebook (Facebook Inc., USA), Twitter (Twitter Inc., USA).
